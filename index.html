Size guide

Choose your fit with confidence.

Rings

Measure the inner diameter of a ring you already wear or wrap a strip of paper around your finger.

Chains

Short: 45cm. Standard: 50cm. Layered: 55cm and above for a relaxed luxury drop.

Bracelets

Measure your wrist snugly, then add 1 to 2 cm for a comfortable premium fit.
